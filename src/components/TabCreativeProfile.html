CREI — Creative Risk & Engagement Index
Xem công thức

Momentum
min(new_creatives/week / genre_median_weekly, 1.5) / 1.5 × 100
×40%

sigmoid(WoW impression change %) × 100 — normalized, 50 = no change
×35%
Channel stability
(1 − HHI) × 100, HHI = Σ(channel_share²) — cao = concentrated
×25%
